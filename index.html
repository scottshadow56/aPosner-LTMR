About This Task

A Posner task variant that simultaneously trains semantic long-term memory retrieval and eye movement — two processes that occur together every time you read.

Depending on your spawn band width setting, two training modes are available:

• Saccadic training (wide band, recommended) — stimuli appear far from centre, training the rapid eye movements you make when scanning text.

• UFOV training (narrow band, experimental) — keep your gaze on the centre dot and try to read stimuli in your periphery.

Controls: J = Yes  |  F = No  |  Space = Next  |  Esc = Exit

Alt — toggle HUD during gameplay  |  Ctrl (hold) — review last 3 completed trials (stimulus, your answer, correct answer)
