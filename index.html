About This Task

A Posner task variant that simultaneously trains semantic long-term memory retrieval and eye movement — two processes that occur together every time you read.

Depending on your spawn band width setting, this app trains two different things:

• Saccadic training (wide band) — stimuli appear near the screen edges, training the eye movements you make when scanning text.

• UFOV training (narrow band) — stimuli appear close to centre. Keep your gaze fixed on the dot and read in your periphery — this expands your useful field of view over time, letting you take in more of a page without moving your eyes.

Controls: J = Yes  |  F = No  |  Space = Next  |  Esc = Exit
